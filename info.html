The Tonal Weather Accessibility Project is a dynamic application dedicated to delivering accurate weather data through the use of auditory tones. Pitch of the generated tone communicates to the user the current temprature of specified location. In regards to Wind KPH - The generated tone represents wind speed via the gain of a pink noise oscillator. T.W.A.P's primary goal is to provide a more robust instance of accessible content via hypertext protocol. Weather data kindly provided by Weather Underground. Special thanks to Flocking JS for providing the synthesis framework.
